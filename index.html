Game Rules

1. Choose your HERO and ENEMY

2. Draw and play cards to attack, heal, and apply effects.

3. For ATTACK cards only, use DODGED cards to prevent damage.

4. Limit to one ATTACK, BOMB, CRITICAL per turn.

5. Game WIN when your enemy HP hits zero before yours.

6. Game DRAW either both HP to zero or deck run out.
