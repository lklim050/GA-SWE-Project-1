
Features and flow

0. Creations
	a. deck Creations - 20 cards containing the following types
		i. Attack: 10cards (does -HP on enemy based on ATK stats)
		ii. Dodged: 5cards (Used when Attack card is played to prevent -HP)
		iii. Heal: 3cards (+3HP)
		iv. Critical: 2cards (cannot be dodged)
	b. characters creation based on stats difference
		i. Warrior, HP: 12, ATK: 2-4, SPD: 1
		ii. Mage, HP: 11, ATK: 2-5, SPD: 2
		iii. Ninja, HP: 10, ATK: 2-6, SPD: 3
	c. deck Shuffle 
	d. 


1. Initializing
	a. check player chosen character
	b. deckShuffle() 
	c. deal cards (start hand 3, max 5)
	d. check player turn based on speed stats

	
2. Player turn
	a. Draw 1 card
	b. Play 1 card 
		i. Attack card: 
			> only once per turn
			> -HP (enemy) based on character atk point
			> can be resolved by enemy dodge card
		ii. Dodged card:
			> only be used during the other player turn
			> only be used to resolve Attack card
			> no -HP from Attack card
		iii. Heal card:
			> recover +3HP (player)
		iv. Critical card:
			> only once per turn
			> -HP (enemy) based on character max atk point
			> cannot be resolved by enemy dodge card
	c. End turn
	d. Switch turn

3. Check and Update
	a. Check Winner when one of the player HP reaches 0
	b. Update result and message after every card resolved
	c. Update discard pile information
	d. Check player turn and switch
	e. Check if deck is empty, shuffle back remaining cards from discard pile and create new content
